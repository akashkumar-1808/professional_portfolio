AI-Powered Smart Traffic Control System

A research project that uses computer vision and reinforcement learning to replace fixed-cycle traffic signals with an intelligent, adaptive system. It streams live footage from existing CCTV cameras, runs YOLOv8 to detect and classify vehicles in real time, and feeds data into a PPO/DQN reinforcement learning agent that dynamically adjusts signal timings.

Emergency vehicle auto-prioritization
Multi-intersection coordination
Designed for Indian road conditions
No new cameras needed — uses existing CCTV
30-40% Reduction in avg wait time
60% Faster emergency clearance

Targeted for R&D incubation, DST/SERB grant applications, and municipal pilot deployment.
